# Privacy Policy

AwayLock runs locally on your Mac.

## Data AwayLock Uses

- Paired or known Bluetooth device names and identifiers visible to macOS.
- Bluetooth RSSI values used to estimate whether the selected device is nearby.
- Local app settings stored in `UserDefaults`.
- Recent in-memory event logs shown in the app.

## Data Collection

AwayLock does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Network Access

AwayLock only opens external links when you explicitly click them, such as the optional Buy Me a Coffee support link.

## Permissions

AwayLock may request:

- Bluetooth access, to scan nearby BLE devices.
- Accessibility or Apple Events permissions, to trigger the macOS lock shortcut.
